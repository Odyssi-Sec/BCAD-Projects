************************************************************
* 1.  OVERVIEW
************************************************************
*  The Dewey Decimal System Educational Application is a user-friendly tool designed to help users learn and practice the Dewey Decimal Classification System,
*  a widely used library organization system. This application is specifically developed to enhance the understanding of the call number ordering system used in libraries for easy book retrieval.
*  Users can interact with the application to practice arranging call numbers correctly, track their progress, and complete timed challenges for efficiency improvement.
*  The application also keeps a record of users' history for reference.

The application provides the following functionality:

- Select "Numerical Order" mode to practice arranging call numbers in numerical order.

* 5.  IDENTIFYING AREAS PAGE AND OBJECTIVE
************************************************************
The Identifying Areas page is a part of the Dewey Decimal System Educational Application. It focuses on helping users identify Dewey Decimal Categories and their corresponding codes. Here's the objective of the Identifying Areas page:

**Objective: Identify Dewey Decimal Categories**

In this activity, you will be presented with Dewey Decimal numbers and categories. Your goal is to match the Dewey Decimal numbers with their corresponding categories.

Steps:
1. Examine the Dewey Decimal categories and numbers provided on the page.
2. Select a Dewey Decimal number (left) and match it with the correct category (right).
3. Continue matching until you have correctly matched 4 pairs to earn points.
4. The codes and categories will switch every 4th attempt, so be prepared for new challenges.
5. Once you have matched 4 pairs, you can choose to proceed to the next question or reset the game.

Have fun learning the Dewey Decimal System!

Dewey Decimal Dictionary:
- 000 - Generalities
- 100 - Philosophy & Psychology
- 200 - Religion
- 300 - Social Sciences
- 400 - Language
- 500 - Natural Sciences & Mathematics
- 600 - Technology
- 700 - Arts & Recreation
- 800 - Literature
- 900 - History & Geography
